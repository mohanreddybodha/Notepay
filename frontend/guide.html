User Guide
Welcome to Notepay! This comprehensive guide details every feature available in the app and precisely how to use them.
Getting Started
  • Login & Registration: Enter your 10-digit mobile number on the login screen. You will receive an OTP via SMS. After entering the OTP, you can set up your profile.
  • Dashboard: After logging in, you will land on the Dashboard. This shows all the events you are currently participating in.
  • Profile Settings: Tap the profile icon on the top right of the dashboard. Here, you can toggle Dark Mode, click your name to Edit your Profile (Name & Gender), and log out of the app.
Creating & Joining Events
  • Create an Event: On your dashboard, tap the + New Event button at the bottom of the screen, then choose Create Event. Give your event a name and description. You will automatically become the Organizer.
  • Find the Invite Code: Open your event. Tap the three vertical dots (⋮) in the top-right corner, then tap Code Settings. Share this 6-letter code with friends. Organizers can also regenerate the code here.
  • Join an Event: On your dashboard, tap the + New Event button, then choose Join by Event Code. Paste the Invite Code to join instantly!
Event Settings & Members
Inside any event, tap the three vertical dots (⋮) in the top-right corner to access powerful tools:
  • Members: Tap here to see all participants. Organizers can tap a member's name to Restrict them or transfer ownership. Restricted members cannot view or add entries.
  • Public Access: Turn this ON to generate a Share View Link. Anyone with this link can view the event ledger in a read-only state without logging in.
  • Download PDF Report: Organizers can tap this to generate and download a comprehensive, professional PDF summary of all collections and expenses.
Logging Finances
  • Collections & Expenses: Tap the "+" button in the Collections or Expenses tab to add an entry. The app calculates your Live Balance automatically.
  • Custom Columns: Need to track extra info like "House Number"? When adding a new entry, scroll down to the "Custom Fields" section and tap the + Add Column button.
  • Hide Columns: To keep your view clean, tap the three vertical dots (⋮) in the event header and choose Hide Collections Table or Hide Expenses Table.
Viewing, Sorting & Theater Mode
  • Summary Tab: At the bottom of the event screen, tap the Summary tab to see a breakdown of your event's health, top donors, and your personal contributions.
  • Search & Filter: Tap the Search bar in the top-right to find specific names or entries. Tap the Filter icon (slider lines) next to the search bar to Sort By Time, Amount, or Name, and to filter only "My Entries".
  • Theater Mode: For a wide, spreadsheet-like view, simply rotate your phone horizontally, OR tap the circular rotate icon next to the "+" button. This opens a wide-screen view where you can effortlessly switch between Collections, Expenses, and Summary tabs.
The AI Assistant
Notepay features an intelligent assistant right inside your event chat!
  • How to use: Open the event Chat tab. Type @ai followed by your question and hit send.
  • Financial Summaries: Ask the AI questions like "@ai who donated the most?" or "@ai what is our current remaining balance?"
  • Event Advice: Ask questions like "@ai how should I politely ask members for their pending donations?"
Member Roles Explained
Organizer
The creator of the event. They have full control: they can edit or delete the event, generate PDFs, modify any financial records, regenerate invite codes, and restrict members.
Collector
A standard member. They can read all event data, send chat messages, and add new Donations or Expenses. They can only edit or delete their own entries.
Restricted
A member whose access has been paused by the Organizer. They cannot see the event ledger, chat, or add entries until the Organizer restores their access.
