User Guide
Welcome to Notepay! This guide will help you understand how to organize events, track shared expenses, and manage your groups effortlessly.
Getting Started
Notepay relies on secure, password-less authentication using your mobile number.
  • Login & Registration: Enter your 10-digit mobile number on the login screen. You will receive a secure OTP via SMS. After entering the OTP, you can set up your profile name.
  • Dashboard: After logging in, you will land on the Dashboard. This is your command center where you can see all the events you are currently participating in.
Creating & Joining Events
Events are dedicated spaces where you and your friends can track collections and expenses.
  • Create an Event: Tap the "Create" button on your dashboard. Give your event a name and description. You will automatically become the Organizer.
  • Invite Members: Open your event and tap the settings or header to view the Invite Code. Share this secure 6-letter code with your friends.
  • Join an Event: Tap the "Join" button on the dashboard and paste the Invite Code your friend gave you. You will instantly be added to their event!
Member Roles Explained
Notepay has a strict, simple permissions system to keep your event data safe.
Organizer
The creator of the event. They have full control: they can edit or delete the event, modify any financial records, generate new invite codes, and manage member access.
Collector
A standard member. They can read all event data, send chat messages, and add new Donations or Expenses. They can only edit or delete their own entries.
Restricted
A member whose access has been paused by the Organizer. They cannot see the event ledger, chat, or add entries until the Organizer restores their access.
Logging Finances
Notepay tracks exactly how much money has been collected and how much has been spent, giving you a live remaining balance.
  • Collections (Donations): When someone gives money towards the event, tap the "+" button in the Collections tab. Enter the donor's name and the amount.
  • Expenses (Spent): When money is spent on event activities, tap the "+" button in the Expenses tab. Enter a description and the amount.
  • Live Balance: The app automatically calculates the difference between total collections and total expenses, displaying the Remaining Balance at the top of the event page.
The AI Assistant
Notepay features an intelligent assistant right inside your event chat to help you manage the event!
  • How to use: Open the event Chat tab. Type @ai followed by your question and hit send.
  • Financial Summaries: Ask the AI questions like "@ai who donated the most?" or "@ai what is our current remaining balance?" The AI instantly reads the ledger and replies!
  • Event Advice: You can also ask the AI for organizational tips! Ask questions like "@ai how should I politely ask members for their pending donations?"
Security Limits: To protect the platform, you can only send 5 OTP requests per hour, and a maximum of 10 AI queries per hour. Chat messages are also limited to 20 per minute.
