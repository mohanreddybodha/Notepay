Getting Started
Login with OTP: Enter your 10-digit mobile number on the login screen and tap Send OTP. Enter the 6-digit OTP received via SMS to verify your identity and sign in.
Profile Setup (first time): New users are automatically taken to the Profile Setup screen after OTP verification. Enter your full name, select your gender, then tap Continue to complete registration.
Dashboard: After login you land on the Dashboard, which shows three swipeable tabs — My Events (events you created), Shared Events (events you joined via code), and Visited Events (public events you have viewed). Swipe left or right to switch, or tap the tab names.
Search events: Use the search bar at the top of the dashboard to instantly find any event by name or description.
Creating & Joining Events
Create an event: Tap the New Event button at the bottom of the Dashboard (or Create Event in the sidebar). Fill in the event name, an optional description, an optional funding goal, and optional date, then tap Create. You automatically become the Organizer.
Get the invite code: Open your event → tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner → tap Event Joining Code. You will see the 16-character join code and a copy button. You can also tap Regenerate to create a new one (the old code stops working immediately).
Join an event: Tap the New Event button → tap Join by Event Code (or Join by Code in the sidebar). Enter the 16-character invite code shared by the organizer. You instantly join as a Collector.
Edit an event: From the Dashboard, tap the edit icon on any event card you own to update the event name, description, goal amount, or date.
Inside an Event — Three Tabs
Collections tab: Tracks every donation or payment received. Each row shows the donor's name, amount (₹), who collected it, and the timestamp. This is the core income ledger of your event.
Expenses tab: Tracks every cost or expenditure. Each row shows a description, the amount spent, who added it, and the timestamp. This is the spending ledger.
Summary tab: A live financial snapshot — total collected, total spent, remaining balance, top donors, and a per-member contribution breakdown. It updates automatically as entries are added.
Event info bar: Below the app bar, you will see the event description, date, and a Members badge. Tap Members to view all current members and their roles.
Adding & Managing Entries
Add a new entry: At the bottom of the Collections or Expenses table, tap the green + New entry row. A form slides up where you type the donor name and optional amount. Tap Save or Save & Next to add and immediately open a blank form for the next entry.
Edit an entry: Tap any existing row in the table to open its edit form. Update the name, amount, or any custom fields, then tap Save. Note: Collectors can only edit their own entries. Organizers can edit any entry.
Delete an entry: While editing, tap the Delete option and confirm in the popup. Collectors can only delete their own entries. Organizers can delete any entry.
Duplicate warning: If you add an entry with the same name and amount as an existing one, Notepay shows a warning popup. Tap Add Anyway to proceed or Cancel to discard.
Receive Donations (UPI QR & AI)
Set up your UPI details: As an Organizer, tap ⋮ → Receive Donations (QR). Enter your UPI ID and UPI Registered Name exactly as they appear in your bank app. Choose which donor fields are required (e.g. phone, address, custom fields).
Share the collection link: Copy the generated public donation link and share it with donors via WhatsApp, SMS, or any channel. Donors who open the link see your event name, organizer name, and a dynamically generated UPI QR code — no app installation required.
Donor pays and uploads proof: After paying via any UPI app, the donor uploads a screenshot of their payment receipt on the same page.
AI auto-verification: Notepay's AI immediately reads the uploaded screenshot — it checks that the receiver name matches your UPI Registered Name and extracts the amount and date. If the receipt is valid, the entry is automatically created in your event's Collections tab.
Manual fallback: If AI verification fails or cannot extract details (e.g. blurry screenshot), the donor is shown a manual form to type their name, amount, and required fields. All entries remain under your full Organizer control.
Custom Columns
Add a column: In the Collections or Expenses tab, look just below the search bar and find the donor or expense count. Tap the + Column button next to it. Enter a column name (e.g. "House No.", "Ward No."), choose a display width — Narrow, Medium, or Wide — then tap Add Column. The column appears instantly in the table.
Edit or hide a column: Tap the name of any custom column header directly in the table. A settings sheet slides up. You can rename it, change its width, or tap Hide to remove it from view without losing any data.
Restore or delete hidden columns: Tap the + Column button → tap the Hidden Cols button. All previously hidden columns are listed. Tap Restore to bring one back, or tap the red Delete button to permanently remove the column and all its data.
Search, Sort & Filter
Search: At the top of the Collections or Expenses tab, use the Search donors… or Search expenses… input to find entries as you type. Results filter instantly.
Sort & filter: Tap the filter icon (three horizontal sliders) inside the search bar. A modal opens with sorting options: Time Newest First, Time Oldest First, Amount High to Low, Amount Low to High, and Name A to Z.
My Entries Only: In the same filter modal, toggle My Entries Only to show only the entries that you personally added, hiding all other collectors' data from view.
Theater Mode (Wide View)
Enter Theater Mode: Tap the Maximize button next to the "Total" amount on any table, or simply rotate your phone to landscape orientation. The screen expands to a wide spreadsheet view that shows all columns at once.
Navigate in Theater Mode: Use the tabs at the top to switch between Collections, Expenses, and Summary. The search bar and filter icon remain available. Tap Close in the top corner to return to the normal view.
Chat & AI Assistant
Open Chat: Inside any event, tap the chat bubble icon floating at the bottom-right of the screen. A chat drawer slides open from the right side.
Send messages: Type in the text field at the bottom and tap send. Long-press any message to reply directly to it or to react with an emoji.
Use the AI Assistant: Type @ai followed by your question and send it — for example, "@ai who donated the most?" or "@ai what is the remaining balance?". The AI reads the event's live financial data and replies directly in the chat thread.
Organizer Menu (⋮)
Rename Event: Change the event name and description at any time.
Event Joining Code: View the invite code, copy it to clipboard, or regenerate a new code. The old code instantly stops working when regenerated.
Members: See all event members with their roles. Tap any member's name to Restrict them (blocks all access), Promote to Organizer, or Demote an existing organizer back to Collector.
Receive Donations (QR): Configure your UPI ID, UPI Registered Name, and which donor fields are required for the public donation link.
Public Access: Toggle ON to generate a read-only shareable link. Anyone with the link can view event data without joining. Toggle OFF to make the event private.
Share View Link: Appears only when Public Access is ON. Copies the public read-only link to your clipboard.
Download PDF Report: Generates a professionally formatted PDF containing all collections, expenses, and a financial summary for the event.
Hide / Show tables: Temporarily hide the Collections or Expenses table from the event view. Tap again to restore visibility.
Deactivate Event: Locks the event. All Collectors are immediately blocked. The invite code changes when the event is reactivated.
Delete Event: Permanently deletes the event and all its data. This action cannot be undone.
Collector Menu
Exit Event: If you are a Collector, the three dots (⋮) menu shows a single option — Exit Event. This removes you from the event. Your previous entries will remain in the ledger. You will need a new invite code to rejoin.
Member Roles
Organizer
The creator or a promoted member. Has full control — can rename, delete, deactivate, download PDF, manage members, toggle public access, edit or delete any entry, and regenerate invite codes.
Collector
A member who joined via invite code. Can view all data, add new entries, edit or delete their own entries, send chat messages, and use the AI assistant.
Restricted
A Collector who has been blocked by the Organizer. They cannot view event data, add entries, or use the chat. Only the Organizer can restore their access.
Profile & Help
Open Profile: On the dashboard, tap your circular avatar in the top-right corner to open the Profile page.
Edit Name: On the Profile page, tap your name or the edit badge on your avatar to update your display name. Your new name appears across all events immediately.
Dark Mode: Under the Preferences section on the Profile page, toggle Dark Mode on or off. Your preference is saved automatically and applied across all pages.
Submit Feedback: Under Help & Support, tap Submit Feedback to report bugs, request features, or share your thoughts with the team.
