Privacy Policy
Last Updated: May 2026
Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Notepay collects, how it is used, and who can see it.
1. What Data We Collect
To provide our services, Notepay collects the following personal information:
  • Phone Number: Used exclusively for secure OTP authentication (via Firebase) and linking you to events.
  • Profile Details: Your Name and Gender, which help identify you within the app.
  • App Data: Expenses, donations, custom column data, and event information you manually enter.
2. How We Use Your Data
We use your data solely to operate the Notepay platform. Your phone number verifies your identity. Your name and financial entries are used to calculate totals and display records within the specific events you join. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.
3. Who Can See Your Data
Notepay is a collaborative platform. Because of this:
  • When you join an Event, the Organizer and other members of that specific event can see your Name, Role, and the financial entries associated with you.
  • Your Phone Number may be visible to Event Organizers to help them manage their groups and verify members.
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties, marketers, or data brokers.
4. Data Security
We take security seriously. All personal information and event data are securely stored using industry-standard backend services (Google Firebase). We implement strict database rules to ensure users can only access data for events they are authorized to view.
5. Data Retention & Deletion
Your data is kept as long as your account is active. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated personal data from our servers, you may contact the developer or use the in-app deletion tools (when available).
6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify users within the app. Your continued use of Notepay indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
